About

How was the project created ?

spm has been created by Hervé Ehrhart and Adrien Guémy, 2 French entrepreneurs who met at 42, a programming school based in Paris.


When they started as freelances, they noticed the power of collaborative registries and the similarities from one front-end project to another from a project management perspective.


Very quickly, what had been designed as an internal optimization tool made sense for their designers and developers friends. Feeling that the community was lacking such a tool, they decided to pause their business to publish an open registry relevant for visual components and interfaces.
